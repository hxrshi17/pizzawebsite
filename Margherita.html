Margherita Pizza


Description

Indulge in the simplicity and perfection of our Margherita pizza. A classic Neapolitan creation, this pizza features a harmonious combination of fresh ingredients, including vine-ripened tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, fragrant basil leaves, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, all resting atop our signature hand-stretched dough. Every bite is a celebration of tradition and flavor, transporting you to the streets of Naples with its authentic taste.


Ingredients

  • Hand-stretched pizza dough
  • Fresh vine-ripened tomatoes
  • Creamy mozzarella cheese
  • Fragrant basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Pinch of sea salt
