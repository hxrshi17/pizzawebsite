Neapolitan Pizza


Description

Indulge in the authentic flavors of Naples with our Neapolitan Pizza. Crafted with expertise and passion, our Neapolitan pizza captures the essence of Italian culinary tradition. Each bite is a symphony of fresh ingredients, savory sauce, and the perfect balance of cheese. With a thin, crispy crust and a soft, chewy center, every slice will transport you to the bustling streets of Naples.


Ingredients

  • Dough: Flour, Water, Yeast, Salt, Olive Oil
  • Sauce: San Marzano Tomatoes, Salt, Olive Oil, Fresh Basil
  • Cheese: Fresh Mozzarella
  • Toppings (Optional): Basil Leaves, Sliced Tomatoes, Garlic, Anchovies, Olives, Capers

  • Calories: Approximately 250-300 kcal
  • Total Fat: 8-10g
  • Saturated Fat: 3-4g
  • Cholesterol: 15-20mg
  • Sodium: 500-600mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 30-35g
  • Dietary Fiber: 2-3g
  • Sugars: 2-3g
  • Protein: 10-12g
